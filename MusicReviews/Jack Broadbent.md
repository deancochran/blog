## Review:
Jack is dope. This guy has some serious vocals. My favorite track has got to be his major hit, “on the road again,” and no it’s not the cover.

Something about his slide guitar technique stands out from others. Maybe it’s the fact he uses a flask as a slide.

His newest album ‘Ride’ is great too. His signature delta crunch guitar tone just adds to his style.
